Help Us Understand the Link Between Hearing & Alzheimer’s

If you’re 60+ and don’t wear a hearing aid, you could earn £150 by volunteering in safe NHS research. Whether you are volunteering yourself or registering a loved one with Alzheimer’s, please use call us or use this form to get in touch.

Who Can Volunteer?
  • Age 60+
  • Do NOT wear a hearing aid
What’s Involved?
  • Receive compensation of £150 + travel costs
  • Total time: 2–3 hours
  • Safe, non-invasive brainwave test
